Custom software doesn't have to be expensive to operate. The Xcelerate Women platform — onboarding, billing, directory, and automated third-party syncs — runs for about $17/month on Fly.io. Most SaaS stacks doing a fraction of the job cost ten times that, and you never own them.

How I work

  1. Discovery We talk through your current setup — what's working, what isn't, and what you actually need. No pitch, just a conversation.
  2. Scoping I put together a clear proposal: what gets built, what it costs, and what done looks like. No surprises.
  3. Build Iterative development with regular check-ins. You see progress early and often, and can weigh in at every step.
  4. Handoff You own the codebase and infrastructure outright. I stay available for maintenance, future work, or questions.

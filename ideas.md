# Calvert Labs — Ideas & Growth Strategy

## Website Growth

### Positioning
- Primary audience: small business decision makers evaluating build vs. buy
- Secondary audience: developers who might refer work or collaborate
- Differentiator: boutique, direct relationship, you own the output — not a faceless agency

### What's working
- Clean, minimal aesthetic — intentional contrast to bloated agency sites
- $17/month stat is a strong anchor for the build vs. buy argument
- Moira Rose / personality adds memorabilia and memorability for a solo shop
- Screenshots with lightbox give proof without being flashy

### Ideas to explore
- Case study: write up the Xcelerate Women build in more depth — the before/after (Webflow + Memberstack → custom) is a compelling story
- Testimonial: a quote from the Xcelerate Women client would add trust for decision makers
- Referral/word of mouth: think through how to make it easy for happy clients to refer work
- SEO: currently zero — even basic meta tags and an og:image could help discoverability

### Things to leave alone
- No blog (maintenance burden, goes stale)
- No auto-deploy from GitHub (site is stupidly simple by design)
- No agency-speak or compliance/security boilerplate
